Privacy Policy – Boot Time

Developer: Arnaldo Junior (I9Android)

The Boot Time app is a utility that monitors the device reboot to display useful information to the user. It does not require login nor collect sensitive data.

1. Information Collection

Boot Time does not collect personally identifiable information. Only local data and statistics from the device itself are used, such as boot time and startup duration.

2. Use of Information

  • Show the system boot time;
  • Store startup statistics locally (without sending to the cloud).

3. Requested Permissions

  • Access to device state;
  • Boot permissions (to start automatically after reboot).

4. Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data leakage. All processing is done locally on the device.

5. Your Rights

You can uninstall the app at any time. No data is kept on external servers.

Privacy Policy – DinheirU

Developer/Controller: Arnaldo Junior (I9Android)

DinheirU is a real-time budgeting and financial control app, allowing simultaneous entries by multiple family members in the same family (or individually).

1. Services and Technologies Used

  • Firebase Authentication (mandatory login);
  • Realtime Database and Cloud Firestore (data);
  • Firebase Storage (receipts);
  • Firebase Functions (automatic processes);
  • Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics (metrics and errors);
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications);
  • Google AdMob (ads – banners and videos);
  • Google Play Billing (license without ads).

2. Data We Collect

  • Account name and email;
  • Financial transaction data (values, categories, attachments);
  • Usage and crash data (Analytics and Crashlytics);
  • Notification tokens (push);
  • License purchase status (no card details – processed by Google).

3. Purpose of Use

  • Manage accounts and families;
  • Synchronize expenses in real time;
  • Display ads (when applicable);
  • Allow removal of ads via license;
  • Send relevant notifications.

4. Storage and Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest (TLS and Google Cloud);
  • Security rules in Firebase ensuring access only to authorized members.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time via email: arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy – Biblix

Last update: September 22, 2025

Summary

  • We collect Firebase account identifiers (UID, email, name, optional photo), quiz progress (levels, score, coins, achievements), usage events and ad identifiers provided by the device.
  • We use this data to authenticate you, sync your progress, provide limited personalization, improve quality through metrics and telemetry, send relevant notifications and monetize the app with ads.
  • We share data only with essential providers such as Google Firebase, Google Sign-In and Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), following their policies.
  • You may exercise rights of access, correction, portability, withdrawal of consent and others under the LGPD, GDPR and CCPA.
  • Request deletion or portability via arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com; removal may erase progress, levels, coins and achievements.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following data when you use Biblix:

  • Account data: Firebase UID, email address, name and an optional profile photo provided by you or through Google Sign-In.
  • Progress data: current level, score, coins, unlocked achievements and history of quizzes taken.
  • Usage events and telemetry: interactions with quizzes, screens accessed, timestamps, performance metrics and crashes logged by Firebase Analytics.
  • Identifiers and notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens, device identifiers and advertising IDs provided by the operating system.
  • Technical metadata: device model, operating system, language and logs needed for support and fraud prevention.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information and ask that you do not send data beyond what is necessary.

2. How we use the information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate your account via email/password or Google Sign-In through Firebase Auth.
  • Save and sync progress, levels, coins and achievements in Cloud Firestore.
  • Provide limited personalization (for example, recommending quizzes based on your progress) and keep your profile up to date.
  • Measure performance, detect errors and understand usage metrics through Firebase Analytics.
  • Send notifications about new quizzes, reminders or bonuses via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Display and measure ads from Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to sustain monetization.

3. Legal bases under the LGPD, GDPR and CCPA

Depending on your location and interaction with the app, we process personal data based on:

  • Consent: for notifications, personalized ads when applicable and the use of advertising identifiers.
  • Performance of a contract: to provide Biblix, authenticate you, maintain your progress and record achievements.
  • Legitimate interest: to improve the app, prevent fraud, ensure security and measure essential metrics without harming your rights.
  • Legal obligations: to respond to requests from authorities or comply with applicable laws, including consumer rights.

You can revoke consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

4. Sharing with third parties

We share your data only with:

  • Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Cloud Messaging) – see the Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Google Sign-In and Google Mobile Ads/AdMob) – see the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Technical support and infrastructure providers contracted to operate Biblix under confidentiality obligations.

We may also share aggregated or anonymized data, without identifying you, for reporting and analytics.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain your data while your account remains active and for up to 24 months after the last interaction for backup, accounting and legal obligations. Event logs and advertising identifiers are kept for as long as needed for usage analysis, usually up to 13 months or as allowed by the platforms. You may request deletion, anonymization or portability by emailing arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com. After confirming your identity, we will delete or export the data within a reasonable time and inform you of impacts such as permanent loss of progress, levels, coins and achievements.

6. Children and minors

Biblix is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13 (or the equivalent age under local law) without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a minor has provided data without authorization, contact us at arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com to request removal or blocking.

7. Security

We apply reasonable measures such as secure authentication, encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls and anomaly monitoring within Google Firebase services. Despite our efforts, no storage or transmission method is 100% secure; we recommend keeping your credentials confidential and updating your devices regularly.

8. Your rights

Depending on the jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Confirm whether we process your data and access copies.
  • Correct incomplete, inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Request deletion, anonymization or blocking of unnecessary or excessive data.
  • Request portability to another provider when technically feasible.
  • Withdraw consent or opt out of sharing data with third parties for advertising purposes under the CCPA.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest and request review of relevant automated decisions.

To exercise any right, send your request to arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com. We may ask for additional information to confirm your identity before responding.

9. International transfers

Google Firebase and Google services may process and store your data in data centers located outside your country of residence. We use standard contractual safeguards, technical controls and policies aligned with the LGPD, GDPR and CCPA requirements to protect your data during international transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in Biblix or in legal requirements. When relevant changes occur, we will notify you within the app, by email or through another appropriate method before the new version takes effect. The “Last update” date will be adjusted accordingly.

11. Contact

For questions, requests or complaints related to privacy, write to arnaldo.araujo.dev@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time and work to resolve your inquiries.
